I think the ability to simulate and predict has become indispensable for modern engineering challenges that need rapid iteration and deep physical insight. Moreover, we are at an inflection point where traditional numerical methods are converging with deep learning tools, opening entirely new promising paradigms for turbulence modeling. My long-term career aspiration is to lead a team dedicated to developing next-generation simulation and modeling technology. To democratize high-fidelity CFD, making it accessible for rapid design optimization in industry while advancing research in turbulence physics.

I completed dual degree with bachelors in Ocean engineering and integrated masters in Computational engineering from IIT Madras. My research interests lie in high-fidelity turbulence simulations, high performance computing, particularly in application-oriented problems. I am also intrested in data assimilation, neural differentiation based approaches with traditional solvers.
